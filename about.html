Our Mission

At **Unclatter**, we believe that one person's clutter can be another's treasure. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to create a platform that reduces waste, helps those in need, and gives items a second chance. We provide an easy way for people to declutter their homes while supporting charitable causes and sustainable living.

How It Works

We offer a two-pronged approach for your unwanted items:

  • Donate: You can donate items you no longer need. We'll take care of them and find a deserving recipient—whether it's an orphanage, a children's home, or an elderly care facility. Your generosity directly impacts the lives of others.
  • Sell: If you prefer to sell your items at a "throwaway price," we'll purchase them. We then take on the task of repairing, rebuilding, or refurbishing them to give them new life. We sell these revitalized items, and from the proceeds, we give **10% to children's homes, elderly homes, and other charitable organizations.**

What We Accept

We are a one-stop solution for a wide range of goods. We accept almost anything you have, regardless of its condition. Whether it's **new, old, broken, or not functioning**, we're interested!

  • Clothes and accessories
  • Household items and furniture
  • Tools and hardware
  • Electronics and gadgets
  • Farm produce and other perishables
  • ...and much more!

Our Impact

By using Unclatter, you're not just getting rid of things—you're contributing to a sustainable cycle of giving. You help reduce landfill waste, provide essential goods to those who need them most, and support a business model that prioritizes social responsibility. Join us in our journey to create a cleaner, kinder world, one item at a time.
